GARDEN OF ELDON

3 hour long Exhibition by Phoebe Longstaff and Marcus Wagner and their collaborative paintings
10.6.2024
Eldon Gardens Shopping Center
Newcastle Upon Tyne
UK






GARDEN OF ELDON is an opportonistic blink-and-you-miss-it(did you miss it? did you miss meee?)one-time showing of collaborative paintings 
by Phoebe and Marcus in the abandoned shopping centre Eldon Gardens in Newcastle Upon Tyne. These paintings are created to capture blurred 
emotional states and communicate secrets.
