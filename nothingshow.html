The Nothing Show

Artists 
During the summer, 46 artworks were collected and temporarily installed on a floating boat island on the Danube River, in Vienna. The concept was created around the idea to make a joke about the common made up Holy Spirit around work presentations or the exclusivity of art spaces. This fleeting exhibition existed only for a short time, was fully documented and now remains as a video shaped Exhibition, titled „The Nothing Show“.
Through the wish to bring all these works back together and display them within a space, the artworks started to refer back to the original exhibition on the island and its documentation.
The curatorial decision to present them like relics or traces of an event that has already past, felt like the only Logical Solution and puts the attention back to the video. Which is the main „Show“.
The Topic of this project was fake drawn out of a live instagram stream, where we pretended as we would have selected the topic through pure luck. The whole project was in company of a friend, who was choosen as he is not activily involved inside the art scene, but has an interesting art fashion like instagram persona.
